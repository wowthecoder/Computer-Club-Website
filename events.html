Past Events
Annual Code Day 2018: #CodeRace ---7th July 2018

The Annual Code Day is the trademark event of the WMSKL(I) Computer Club. On this day the club meetings will be extended after school until 5.00 pm. The club will organise fun activities for the members to participate in according to the theme, which will change every year. Last year the theme of the event was #CodeRace, where the members will compete with each other to complete a game designed by the Board. The game contained levels of Capture the flag, which requires the members to find the hidden message in the websites, Spot The Mistake In The Code, Solve The Algorithm and more.

Charity Food Sale 2018 ---1st-4th October 2018

The Charity Food Sale was organised by the Computer Club of WMSKL(I) to aid the earthquake and tsunami victims in Indonesia. The terrible 7.5 Mw earthquake struck Sulawesi, Indonesia on the 28th of September and the death toll was approximately 4,300. Homes and buildings were destroyed during the process and tens of thousands were left homeless. We have taken up the initiative to help them and all revenue had gone to charitable organisations that aided the victims. Foods sold include brownies, cakes, biscuits and dessert tarts.

Annual Code Day: #GameCarnival ---16th July 2019
Our much-anticipated Annual Code day is coming soon! This year, the theme of the event is #GameCarnival, which features lots of games made by the club members. Club members will be divided into groups with 5 to 8 people in a group and they will be given about two months to create their game. Games can be created using any programming language and can be put onto any game platform. The games created will be played by all the students in the school and the students will vote to decide the winners of this game-making contest. Prizes include headphones, gaming mouse and VR gaming equipments. This event is exclusively for the members only.

Wanna show off your game-making talents? Here is the chance! Sign up quick to participate!

Club trip: Visit to Google Company Malaysia ---27th August 2019

Ever wonder how Google works? Here is a chance to find out. Join us as we visit the Google Company Malaysia, which is located at Level 20, Axiata Tower, 9, Jalan Stesen Sentral 5, Kuala Lumpur Sentral, 50470 Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur. A Google employee will be our guide and she will explain to us everything about Google. This knowledge and experience is valuable for those who are interested in technology careers as Google is one of the biggest technological companies in the world. This trip is also for club members only.

Only RM30 to go into the core of the most popular browser in the world! Why wait anymore?
